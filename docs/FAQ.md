# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about FileConverter.

## General

### What file formats does FileConverter support?
FileConverter supports **117 file formats** across 4 categories:
- **Documents (43 formats):** PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT, RTF, ODT, HTML, MD, JSON, XML, and more
- **Spreadsheets (17 formats):** XLSX, XLS, CSV, ODS, TSV, and more
- **Presentations (7 formats):** PPTX, PPT, ODP, and more
- **Images (50 formats):** JPG, PNG, WebP, SVG, HEIC, RAW formats, and more

See the [Supported Formats](/docs/supported-formats) page for the complete list.

### Do I need to create an account to use FileConverter?
No, you can use FileConverter without creating an account. However, creating a free account gives you access to:
- Conversion history
- Higher limits
- Additional features

Paid accounts offer even more benefits like unlimited conversions and API access.

### Is FileConverter free to use?
Yes! FileConverter offers a **free tier** with 200 conversions per month. We also offer paid plans (Starter, Professional, Enterprise) with more features, higher limits, and priority processing. See our [Pricing](/pricing) page for details.

### How fast are file conversions?
Conversion speed depends on file size and format:
- **Free tier:** 2-5 minutes
- **Starter:** 1-2 minutes
- **Professional:** 30 seconds
- **Enterprise:** Instant (highest priority)

Simple conversions (image format changes) are typically faster than complex conversions (document format changes).

### What is the maximum file size I can convert?
File size limits vary by subscription tier:
- **Free:** 100MB per file
- **Starter:** 500MB per file
- **Professional:** 2GB per file
- **Enterprise:** 10GB per file (practical limit, though UI shows "Unlimited")

If you need to convert larger files, consider upgrading your plan.

### Can I convert multiple files at once?
Batch conversion is available for **Professional** and **Enterprise** plans. Free and Starter plans support single file conversions.

### Do you support batch conversions?
Yes, batch conversions are available for Professional and Enterprise plans. Upload multiple files and convert them all at once.

## Security & Privacy

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we take security seriously:
- All files are encrypted in transit (HTTPS)
- Files are stored securely
- Files are automatically deleted after processing (24 hours for free tier)
- We never access, read, or share your file contents

See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for more details.

### How long are my files stored?
- **Free tier:** Files are deleted after 24 hours
- **Paid tiers:** Files are stored for 7 days (or as configured)
- **Enterprise:** Custom retention policies available

### Do you access or read my files?
No. We never access, read, or share your file contents. Files are processed automatically and deleted after the retention period.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes, all payments are processed securely through our payment partners. We never store your full payment information.

## Quality & Formatting

### Will my formatting be preserved?
Formatting preservation depends on the source and target formats:
- **Document conversions:** Most formatting is preserved, but complex layouts may vary
- **Spreadsheet conversions:** Data and basic formatting are preserved, complex formulas may need review
- **Presentation conversions:** Content is preserved, animations and transitions are not
- **Image conversions:** Quality is preserved (lossless for PNG, TIFF, BMP)

### Will there be quality loss?
- **Lossless formats:** PNG, TIFF, BMP preserve original quality
- **Lossy formats:** JPG, WEBP may have quality loss (adjustable quality settings)
- **Document conversions:** Formatting may vary slightly between formats

### Can I adjust conversion quality?
Yes, for image conversions you can adjust quality settings:
- **High quality:** Best quality, larger file size
- **Medium quality:** Balanced quality and file size
- **Low quality:** Smaller file size, lower quality

## Billing & Plans

### Can I change plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades, and at the next billing cycle for downgrades.

### What happens if I exceed my limit?
You'll be notified when approaching your limit. You can:
- Upgrade your plan
- Wait until the next billing cycle
- Contact support for a temporary increase (Enterprise)

### Do unused conversions roll over?
No, conversions reset each billing cycle. Consider upgrading if you consistently exceed your limit.

### Are there any hidden fees?
No. The price you see is the price you pay. No setup fees, no transaction fees, no hidden costs.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your plan will remain active until the end of the billing period.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes, we offer a **30-day money-back guarantee** on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund.
